Starting Point

I grew up liking anything that made me curious about how things worked. Over time, I learned that I enjoy working on real problems, not just ideas. I like having something in front of me that I can break down, understand, and improve.

What Matters To Me

I respect people who stay calm when things get difficult and who can think clearly when problems start stacking up. That mindset is something I try to practice in my own work and outside of it.

How I Approach Challenges
