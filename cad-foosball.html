Regulation Foosball Table

Coursework (Team Project - Fall 2023)

Design Methodology

Our team aimed to create a table blending modern aesthetics with user-friendly assembly. We adopted a modular design approach, breaking the table into sub-systems (frame, legs, rods) to simplify installation. My specific contributions focused on the player rod sub-assemblies and the final system integration:

  • Sub-System Design: Modeled the player rods, end caps, and washers, ensuring correct tolerances for bearings and bushings to guarantee smooth gameplay rotation.
  • Assembly Management: Led the final assembly process in SolidWorks, resolving mating constraints between the beech wood frame, aluminum legs, and steel rods.
  • Material Selection: Specified a bill of materials including Aluminum Casting, Solid Beech Wood, and Vinyl finishing.
  • DFM & Standardization: Utilized standard McMaster-Carr hardware to ensure repairability and ease of manufacturing.
